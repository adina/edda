Papers to read
--------------

Everyone should read the following paper for Thursday, Sep 29th:

    Drummond and Wilke, 2008
    http://www.cell.com/abstract/S0092-8674(08)00705-8

The assigned group should read & present these two papers in addition to the
first:
   
    Drummond et al, 2005
    http://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pmc/articles/PMC1242296/

    Kudla et al., 2009
    http://www.sciencemag.org/content/324/5924/255.full

    Zhou et al., 2009
    http://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pmc/articles/PMC2734146/

Group members should consider addressing these questions:

  1. How would you generalize the notion of dS based on these papers?

  2. What implications do these papers have for dN/dS calculations and
  detecting "evolutionary signatures of selection"?
