Reading homework (due Wednesday, midnight Pacific time)
-------------------------------------------------------

Answer the questions in ~short paragraph form:

 1. What are the (your) top three reasons for sequencing the human genome?

 2. Based on the assigned reading as well as your own knowledge, what
    is one novel aspect of the human genome from a genomics standpoint?

 3. Give three reasons why the statement "the human genome contains less
    than 30,000 genes, which means humans aren't even twice as complex as
    nematodes" is inane.

Submit these answers to `this form <https://docs.google.com/spreadsheet/viewform?formkey=dFlfazlDM0JTbXdMTjViNjZqYU1oVGc6MQ>`__ by midnight PST.
